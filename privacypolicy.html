Prelude To Our Privacy Policy

olumbus Locksmith Garage, referred to as the ‘company’ or ‘we’, values the privacy of our customers and is committed to making sure your personal information remains secure.

This policy describes how we do that by explaining our data practices. Namely, how we collect, use, store, protect, and disclose your information. It applies to information we collect:

> On our website

> Via text, email, or electronic communication between you and this website

> From your interactions with our advertisements and applications on third-party websites

> When you interact with our advertising and applications on third-party websites and ervices if those applications or advertising include links to this policy.

Please keep in mind that we collect data for the sole purpose of providing better marketing and/or services to you, furthermore, we do not sell any of the information gathered.

We advise that you carefully review our policies before you make use of our website a nd/or services. By using our site and services, you agree to the data practices described below.

Users Under The Age of 18

The use of our website and services is not aimed at children under the age of 18. We prohibit users under this age from providing their information to us.
Conversely, we do not intentionally collect such information - if we become aware that such information has been provided to us without parental consent, we will delete it. If you believe we have information about a user under 18, please get in touch with us via the contact us page and let us know, so that we may take the proper steps to remove it.

What Information We Gather & How We Collect It

There are different types of information we collect from users of our website, including:
> Personally identifiable information such as your name, mailing address, ZIP code, email address, telephone number and anything else the website collects that is defined as personally identifiable information under applicable laws.
> Non-personally identifiable information such as information about your internet onnection, website usage details, IP address, and information collected through cookies, web beacons or other tracking technologies.

There are different means of collecting this information, including:
> Directly, when you provide it to us at the time of service or through e-mail, or a phone call.
> Automatically, as you navigate and interact with our website.

Data That You Provide To Us

The information you provide to us via our website may include:
> Information you provide by completing forms on our website (such as a contact form.)
> Records and copies of correspondence (such as emails).
> Your responses to surveys we may ask you to complete.
> Details of transactions carried out through our website.
> Search queries you perform on our website.

There are different means of collecting this information, including:
> Directly, when you provide it to us at the time of service or through e-mail, or a phone call.
> Automatically, as you navigate and interact with our website.

Information Collected Through Automatic Data Collection Technologies

When you navigate through and interact with our website, we may use automatic data collection technology to collect certain information about your computer and browsing actions & patterns, including:
> Details of your visit on our site: traffic data, location data, and other communication data, as well as resources you access and use on the website.
> Information about your computer and internet connection: IP address, operating system, and browser type.

We may also employ these technologies to collect information about your website activities over time and across third-party websites or other web services (behavioral tracking.) This information is purely statistical and does not include personal information. However, we may maintain and associate it with the personal information we collect through other means or receive from third parties. This helps us to improve our website and to provide better services by allowing us to:
> Estimate our website audience size and usage patterns
> Keep information about your preferences (which allows us to customize your experience on our site to your individual preferences.)
> Increase the speed of your searches
> Recognize you when you return to our site

The technologies we use to collect this data may include:
> Cookies (or browser cookies): cookies are small files that are placed on your hard drive when you visit a site. They are what allow you to remain logged into a website across sessions and can store user preferences. The privacy-minded may wish to alter their browser settings not to accept cookies. However, please be aware that doing so may limit access to certain parts or functions of our website.
> Flash cookies: certain features of our site may use local stored objects (flash cookies) to collect and store information about preferences and navigation around our site. They are not managed by the same browser settings as browser cookies
> Web beacons: some pages of our website may contain web beacons - small electronic files that permit us, or third parties, to track how many users have visited a page or opened an email for other related website statistics (for example, recording the popularity of certain content & verifying system and server integrity.)
> Please also keep in mind that some of the related and tracking technologies use video and/or screen recording as means of website navigation, which is purely for the purpose of better marketing and providing better service to you. Any possible data recorded will not be sensitive or personal information.
